Can I FI:RE?

What is financial independence?

Financial independence is having enough investment income to cover your cost of living without a job. FI:RE requires building a large enough portfolio to sustainably draw income from investments forever, instead of the conventional retirement strategy of drawing down money as you age.

What is early retirement?

Early retirement refers to the choice to retire before the traditional retirement age of 65, enabled by the income generated from your investments and assets.

Is it possible for me?

Achieving financial independence takes a combination of strong income and deep motivation to build a substantial nest egg through disciplined saving, investing, and lifestyle choices. Use the calculator to determine when you could FI:RE based on your financial situation.
